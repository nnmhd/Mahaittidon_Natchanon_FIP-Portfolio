The Project in depth

Brief
The project involves creating a student showcase website for Fanshawe College's Industry Night 2024. The goal is to highlight the works of emerging designers while providing an intuitive, user-friendly experience.
Audiences & Users
The primary audience includes potential employers, industry professionals, and peers. The site should cater to users looking for a quick and engaging way to explore student portfolios.
Challenge
The main challenge is designing a site that balances both creative expression and functionality, ensuring smooth navigation while showcasing diverse design styles.
Directions
We followed a design-first approach, prioritizing aesthetics while ensuring responsiveness and accessibility across devices. We integrated everything we've learned over the past three terms to reflect a comprehensive view of the program, showcasing both technical and creative skills.
Process
Early Stage
We began with wireframing and prototyping in Figma, focusing on the site's core structure. Development started using HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript to build the foundation.
Middle Stage
We refined the design with user testing and feedback, enhancing functionality and responsiveness. Development continued, adding features and improving accessibility.
Final Stage
In the final stage, we polished the design and optimized performance. Final adjustments were made in HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript to ensure a smooth user experience.
Highlight & Thought
This project encapsulates the essence of the Interactive Media Design program, blending creativity with technical expertise. It represents our growth as designers, with each decision grounded in the lessons learned throughout our journey.
